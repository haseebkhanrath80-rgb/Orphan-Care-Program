Our Commitment

Alkhidmat Foundation's Orphan Care Program

Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan is committed to providing orphan and abandoned children with the support they need to lead a fulfilling life. Currently, over 30,000 children receive quality education, healthcare, nutritious food, clothing, and toys through the Alkhidmat Orphan Care Program.

The Orphan Family Support Program provides financial support for education and everyday expenses to orphan children aged 16 or below, ensuring comprehensive mental, social, and moral growth.

If you would like to make a difference in the lives of these children, please consider donating. Your contribution can help provide education, healthcare, and a brighter future.
